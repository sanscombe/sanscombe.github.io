Lancashire-Yorkshire Model Theory Seminar (LYMoTS)

A regular series of meetings of the model theorists in Leeds, Manchester, and Preston. Supported by the London Mathematical Society.

Program of the 2nd meeting on Saturday the 29th of March 2014 in Leeds

10.30-11.00 Arrival and coffee
11.00-12.00 Mike Prest Interpreting modules in modules
12.00-13.00 Immanuel Halupczok Families of definable sets in the ordered group $\mathbb{Z}$ Abstract
13.00-14.30 Lunch
14.30-15.30 Joel Nagloo On Transformations in the Painlevé family Abstract
15.30-16.00 Coffee
16.00-17.00 Ivan Tomašić Applications of the twisted theorem of Chebotarev Abstract
17.00- Pub and Dinner

Venue: MALL, School of Mathematics, University of Leeds, Leeds.
