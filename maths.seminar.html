UCLan Mathematics Seminar

This is the webpage of the UCLan Mathematics Seminar; a regular seminar run by the Mathematicians based in the Jeremiah Horrocks Institute at the University of Central Lancashire.

Everyone is welcome to attend. The seminar is organised by Sylvy Anscombe and Matt Daws. Please  contact Sylvy  or Matt for more details.
